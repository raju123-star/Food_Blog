Welcome To Love & Lemons

Love & Lemons is a Food Blog page created by Pushparaj Naik Pujari.The blog’s name comes from the fact that where I often finished off with a squeeze of lemon.

The blog was founded in 2011 and has been recognized by prestigious food magazines like Food & Wine,SELF Magazine, and Oprah Magazine. It was named Readers’ Choice Best Cooking Blog by Saveur Magazine in 2014 and won a Saveur Editor’s Choice award in 2016.
